About

The Story

DJ DPAK is Sydney's premier open format turntablist, known for blending hip hop, R&B, neo-soul, Afrobeats, and everything in between with championship-level skill on the decks.

In 2024, he won the inaugural Australian DMC Open Category Championship and represented Australia at the DMC World Finals in Paris. Every weekday, he brings the energy as the Drive Home Mixshow DJ on CADA radio, and on game days, he's the official GameDay DJ for the NSW Waratahs, entertaining 20,000+ rugby fans at Allianz Stadium.

From turntablist battle routines to smooth neo-soul brunch sets, DJ DPAK brings craft, energy, and range to every performance. Whether it's a 50-person cocktail bar or a 20,000-seat stadium, the vibe is always championship level.
